Soleil is a (sunny when the weather is nice) outdoor event space in the heart of Toronto’s Little Portugal neighbourhood. The 200-person capacity space is a creative blank canvas, ready for all your daytime outdoor ideas. Think parties (public and private), live music, markets, outdoor film screenings, food pop-ups, and meetups… the possibilities are endless. It’s a flexible, perfectly located space open to all types of events with a focus on BIPOC and queer programming.
Soleil follows all municipal noise regulations that the city of Toronto enforces. However, if you’d like to submit a noise complaint, please think about why you live and/or work in downtown Toronto and don’t like joy or culture, or call 3-1-1.
